Eligibility Requirements Before You Begin

To successfully apply for a license, ensure you meet the following conditions:

Please follow the steps below to register your practitioner account on the WMA Online Services platform.

  • 1. Valid Professional Registration:
    • You must have valid professional credentials.
  • 2. Tax Compliance:
    • A valid Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) and a Certificate of Tax Clearance are required.
  • 3. Identity Verification:
    • A valid National ID or Passport is required.
    • Recent passport-sized photograph (not older than 6 months).
  • 4. Physical Location:
    • Proof of physical address (utility bill, lease agreement, etc.).

Documents Required

Please have the following documents ready for upload:

  • Professional license certificate
  • Tax clearance certificate
  • National ID or Passport
  • Passport-sized photograph
  • Proof of physical address
